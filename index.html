Executive Summary

Unlike a standard e-commerce site, a premium commercial printing platform must process complex dimensional variables, manage massive graphic uploads, perform automated pre-flight quality checks, and handle tiered B2B invoicing. This proposal delivers exactly that under a single fixed-price agreement, completed within a strict 2–3 month window.
